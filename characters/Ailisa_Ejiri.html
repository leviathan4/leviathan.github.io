Ailisa Ejiri

Spirit: Owl Falcon Spirit-Mehira

Element: Wind

Family: Aria

Ailisa Evere is the mother of Mikaela and the wife of Emil Evere. She was born in the city of Silverburgh and met Emil during his travels as an archaeologist. Ailisa has short brown hair and light brown eyes. She is a natural born leader and a very headstrong person. Once she sets her mind to something, she allows nothing to get in her way. Ailisa also loves meeting and talking to new people, once she starts a conversation, it could go on for a while.

Emil, was employed as an archaeologist and geologist for the military of Bellymena and was tasked with finding a suitable place near Bellymena rich in material resources. He found this location after visiting Silverburgh, and Ailisa came along with him, together they founded Halivaara. Ailisa was a leader for the city as it began, and helped guide it to prosperity.
