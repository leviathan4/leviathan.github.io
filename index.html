What is this World About?

The Federation of Kalance, a large island country in the eastern hemisphere, is where the world of Leviathan takes place. It is filled with beautiful landscapes, charming spirits, and diverse people. The history of this country runs deep with a foundation originating from the Azores and Nathair families, two families that have vastly opposing beliefs but have the common goal to do what they believe to be the most beneficial for the country. Each faction seeks to improve the country in line with its worldview. The Azores believe in the potential of human beings and that people given the right motivations with perseverance and determination can accomplish anything. A country with people like this can be mighty with the right leader. The Nathair are obsessed with strength, wealth, and prestige. Their ideal vision for the country is one where the strong rise and the weak fall. Though, even when something is great, there is likely more that can be done to improve it.
