There have always been two rich and highly influential families that have taken reign over the leadership of the country of Kalance. These two families, the Azores family, and the Nathair family, although they have vastly opposing views, they do what they believe is the most beneficial for the country. The Azores family has always believed in the potential of human beings, that people can have small beginnings, but through perseverance and determination, they can achieve anything.
